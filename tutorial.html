THE MARKET

Tutorial

Sprawled on the floor, you couldn't do anything to potentially defend yourself other than bracing yourself for another hit. A few bullies were punching you and bullying you for being so unbelievably poor. All you have is a 10 dollar note, which with it all you can buy is an apple... whatever you'll use that for. but then you come up with an idea. You are so delightened that you literally jump up and run gleefully run home away from the bullies. buy the apple, sell the apple for more money, repeat the process, you are now rich! You stared at the great raisin toast, dreaming that you would have it one day, you dreamed of not being so poor anymore. You... can become rich...

Hello! This game is called, 'the market' as you can see. The Objective of the game is to reach 1M Penties(the specific currency for this game) and prove the bullies wrong... you can buy the apple buy clicking on the box and scrolling down to find the sell buttons to sell the items to get up to 50% profit off the item. Use this process to reach 1M Penties and beat the game. WARNING: IN AN EVENT DO NOT EXIT GAME SCREEN!
